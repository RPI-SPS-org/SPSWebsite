Welcome to SPS!

The Society of Physics Students is a student-run organization for anyone with an interest in physics. We have weekly meetings covering a range of topics, from games to Q&A panels to talks from professors about their research. We also have weekly Build Team meetings, where we construct physics demonstrations that we perform at outreach events both on and off-campus. Whether you're a physics major or not, SPS is an excellent opportunity to meet like-minded individuals and have fun in the process. If you're interested, feel free to stop by a meeting or reach out!
