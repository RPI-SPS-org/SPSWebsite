RPI SPS Alumni Network

Welcome to the RPI SPS Alumni Network (started in 2025). This provides an excellent way for current students and officers to get in touch with former members of our chapter. If you're looking for advice from one of our former members who have since gone on to academia or industry, feel free to browse the network and reach out!

For Alumni

If you've graduated and you'd like your information to appear in our network, please fill out this form! If you've already filled out the form and want to update your information, no worries! Fill out the form again, and simply mark that you'd like your information to be updated.
