SPS Outreach

The Society of Physics Students is one of the most outward-facing aspects of RPI's physics department. Whenever RPI opens its doors to members of the public, you can bet there will be SPS students welcoming them. At Open House, this includes physics demonstrations, a Q&A panel, and an information table. SPS also regularly attends additional events, such as presentations at local schools.
